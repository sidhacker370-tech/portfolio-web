About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a passionate AI-integrated full stack web developer with a deep fascination for the intersection of artificial intelligence and web development. My journey began with a curiosity about how machines can learn and make intelligent decisions, which led me to explore the world of AI and machine learning.
